Privacy Policy
At Jenlisse Beauty Studio, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to handling your personal information with care, transparency, and respect. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you visit our website or use our services.
1. What We Collect
We may collect the following information when you book an appointment, make a purchase, or contact us:
  • Full name and contact details (email, phone number, delivery address)
  • Order information and appointment details
  • Payment preferences (we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Any notes or requests you provide for your services
2. How We Use Your Data
We use your information to:
  • Process your orders and bookings smoothly
  • Communicate with you about your appointment or order status
  • Personalize your experience and respond to your requests
  • Improve our services and ensure your satisfaction
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements
3. Information Sharing
We value your trust and do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with outside parties, except when:
  • It is necessary to fulfill your order or appointment (e.g., delivery partners)
  • Required by law or government authorities
  • You give us explicit consent
4. Data Security
We employ industry-standard measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or misuse. Only authorized team members have access to your information, and all details are kept confidential.
5. Cookies & Analytics
Our website may use cookies and analytics to enhance your browsing experience and help us understand visitor preferences. You can adjust your browser settings to manage cookies as you wish.
6. Your Rights & Choices
You have the right to:
  • Review or update your personal information
  • Request deletion of your data, except where required for business or legal reasons
  • Opt out of promotional communications at any time
To make changes or requests, please contact us at info@jenlissebeauty.com.
7. Updates to This Policy
We may update this policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
